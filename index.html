Our Story


We are a non-profit organisation that is founded on the strength and resilience of a woman – so it was only natural for us to pursue the empowerment of young and vulnerable girls, starting from our community.

The Groom a Girl Initiative Team are committed to bringing about change and making a visible difference in as many young girls’ lives as we possibly can. We provide assistance to young girls who come from disadvantaged backgrounds, whether they are orphaned or have parents that are unable to care for them due to unfortunate circumstances. We help by providing them with the basic necessities that are very important to every young and developing girl through our year-long Pad-Drive and Toiletry-Drive.

I believe if you put the things that matter the msot first, you will always succeed. Your heart will never lead you astray if you nurture it and feel it with love and positivity. Follow it, always, and shape your mind to listen to the heart not fight it. For that is the secret to a fulfilling life with an abundance of joy and happiness. This is an close as we will get to a peaceful life for all, if we all show love and follow our hearts.

Being part of the development of young girls is an intricate task that requires you to not just physically help them, but also cater to their emotional needs. We aim to instil in them a positive attitude and outlook on life, because after all, despite one’s background and circumstances, you will always be able to reach your goals and prosper in life with a good attitude. So, as part of our initiative, we invest time in helping each one of our girls individually, focusing on their strengths and encouraging them to be the best versions of themselves. This allows them to understand that, despite their circumstances, there is a world filled with opportunity and that they can prosper with the right guidance, mindset, focus and work ethic.

Our long-term goal is to open our own centre where we can continue to operate our initiative on our own grounds, creating a tangible place that a girl in need can run to, as a ‘sister-centre’. The team at Groom a Girl Initiative stand firm in the belief that one is not defined by their past or current circumstances – and more importantly, we want our young girls to know that there is nothing that they cannot achieve, irrespective of the challenges that they have and may continue to face.

Groom A Girl Initiative wishes to empower young girls in making positive life choices through mentoring, educational activities, support systems and nurturance. We aim to empower girls by bringing out their beauty and individual potential from within. We want to help change the lives of many young girls by helping them realize their unlimited power as women, and that opportunities do exist, not because of where they come from but because of who they are.

Help us guide this nation’s future generations of successful women, mothers, and leaders.

“EMPOWERED WOMEN, EMPOWERING WOMEN”
